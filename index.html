About

Shams is a fully qualified driving instructor with over ten years of experience, focused on helping learners become safe, confident drivers as efficiently as possible. Nervous learners

Learning to drive can feel overwhelming, especially if you’re anxious or lacking confidence. Shams takes a calm, understanding approach and adapts lessons to how each learner is feeling in real time. Lessons move at a comfortable pace while encouraging steady progress, helping nervous learners build confidence and independence on the road without feeling rushed or pressured.
